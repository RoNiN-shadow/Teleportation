# Teleport Tracker – 10-Slot Teleportation Mod

## Fast travel anywhere with up to 10 personal checkpoints

This mod introduces a new item called the **Teleport Tracker**, allowing you to create and use up to 10 teleport checkpoints for quick movement across your world.

![Teleport Tracker GIF](https://i.imgur.com/cEr8GKh.gif)

## Features

- Up to 10 teleport slots  
- Instant teleportation between saved checkpoints  
- Per-player saved coordinates  
- Simple and intuitive hotkeys  

## Keybinds

- **K + Number** — Save a checkpoint (1–10)  
- **F + Number** — Teleport to a saved checkpoint  

## How to Obtain (Survival)

After defeating the **Moon Lord**, the Teleport Tracker becomes available for purchase from the **Mechanic NPC**.  

## Notes

- Checkpoint data is currently saved per player  
- Future updates may expand multiplayer syncing and customization
