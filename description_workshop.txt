[h1]Teleport Tracker – 10-Slot Teleportation Mod[/h1]

[h2]Fast travel anywhere with up to 10 personal checkpoints.[/h2]

This mod introduces a new item called the [b]Teleport Tracker[/b], allowing you to create and use up to 10 teleport checkpoints for quick movement across your world.

[img]https://i.imgur.com/cEr8GKh.gif[/img]

[h2]Features[/h2]
[list]
[*]Up to 10 teleport slots
[*]Instant teleportation between saved checkpoints
[*]Per-player saved coordinates
[*]Simple and intuitive hotkeys
[/list]

[h2]Keybinds[/h2]
[list]
[*][b]K + Number[/b] — Save a checkpoint (1–10)
[*][b]F + Number[/b] — Teleport to a saved checkpoint
[/list]

[h2]How to Obtain (Survival)[/h2]
After defeating the [b]Moon Lord[/b], the Teleport Tracker becomes available for purchase from the [b]Mechanic NPC[/b].

[h2]Notes[/h2]
[list]
[*]Checkpoint data is currently saved per player.
[*]Future updates may expand multiplayer syncing and customization.
[/list]
